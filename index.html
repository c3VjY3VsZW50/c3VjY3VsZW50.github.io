Installation

Once you have donwloaded MultiMC and extracted it, follow these steps:

Create a new instance and check "Betas" and scroll to b1.7.3.

After you create the instance, right click it and click "Edit Instance"

Go to Version and click "Add to minecraft.jar"

Locate the patch I linked and add it. DO NOT EXTRACT IT

Click "Launch" and you should be good to go. Please note that singleplayer will not work, so here is a good SMP server you can play on:

betalands.com
